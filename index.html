AI In Action

Real-World AI Examples from India

AI isn't just in science labs — it's in your pocket, on cricket fields, and in hospitals. Students learn through examples they already know.

🏏

Cricket DRS — AI on the Field

In IPL cricket, the Hawk-Eye system uses Computer Vision AI to track ball paths and predict outcomes. It decides OUT or NOT OUT in just 2 seconds — analyzing physics data from each ball in real time. Pure AI, not automation!

Computer Vision
🗺️

Google Maps — Live Routing

Google Maps uses AI to analyze GPS data from millions of phones, real-time traffic speed, accident reports, and historical patterns. It predicts the fastest route NOW and re-routes automatically if conditions change.

Statistical Data
🔓

Face Unlock — 30,000 Points

Your phone's Face ID maps 30,000+ unique points on your face to create a mathematical 'face map'. It unlocks in 0.1 seconds, works in dim light, and can't be fooled by a photo. Different from all 8 billion humans!

Computer Vision
🩺

AI Detecting TB in Rural India

India has a severe shortage of radiologists in rural areas. AI trained on 1M+ chest X-rays can detect Tuberculosis with 90%+ accuracy in under 30 seconds — literally saving lives where no specialist doctor is available.
